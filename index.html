Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about eBai

What marketplaces can I use eBai for?
eBai works for any e-commerce platform including eBay, Amazon, Etsy, Poshmark, Mercari, Facebook Marketplace, and your own online store. Our AI-optimized listings are designed to work everywhere.
How does the photo enhancement work?
We use advanced AI to automatically remove backgrounds and replace them with a professional pink studio backdrop. This creates consistent, eye-catching product photos that increase sales by up to 40%.
What file types are supported?
We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, and HEIC (iPhone photos). Files up to 10MB are accepted.
How quickly are photos processed?
Most photos are enhanced within 5-10 seconds. Complex images may take up to 20 seconds.
Do credits expire?
No, credits never expire. Use them whenever you need them.
Is there a watermark on enhanced images?
Yes, a small "ebai.me" watermark is added to the bottom-right corner. It's designed to be unobtrusive while promoting our service.
